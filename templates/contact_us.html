Current UMW Students & Faculty:


The Honors Program is located in duPont Hall Room 210. This space is available for all honors students as a gathering or studying location.


Dr. Slunt, Director of the Honors Program, has an office in duPont 210 as well as in Jepson 337 (Chemistry office).


Questions about the Honors Program can be directed to Kelli Slunt (kslunt@umw.edu) at 540-654-1716 (Honor Program Office) or 540-654-1406 (Chemistry office). In addition, Jeanne Campbell (jcampbe2@umw.edu), administrative assistant for the Honors Program can field questions at 540-654-1592.


Questions for students or suggestions about student events for the honors program can be directed to the Student Advisory Committee for the UMW Honors Program (HSAC), honorssac@gmail.com.
